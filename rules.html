Server Rules

Violations on the server are classified into three categories based on their severity. Sanctions will be applied according to the seriousness of each violation.

  1. Severe
    • Using hacks: any unauthorized modification to gain unfair advantages (e.g., X-ray, fly, speed, etc.). If you wish to use a specific client/mod, consult staff beforehand.
    • Use of autoclicker for PvP: the use of autoclicker is only allowed for farming.
    • Exploiting bugs or glitches: taking advantage of game bugs to gain a clear advantage over other players.
    • Extreme offensive language: comments that directly attack a player's health, identity, or personal situations, such as wishing death on someone.
  2. Moderate
    • Toxic behavior: conduct that, while not direct PvP infractions, negatively affects other players.
    • Offensive language: insults or inappropriate language without reaching extreme attacks.
    • Inappropriate builds: structures that affect the server's performance or are disruptive to other players.
    • Impersonating admin/staff: we do not allow usernames similar to the owner, administrators, or mods. The player will have 30 days to change their name; if not, they may be banned until further notice.
    • Inappropriate skins or names: skins or names with offensive, sexually explicit, political, or otherwise inappropriate content.
  3. Minor
    • Spam: repetitive messages in the chat that disrupt conversation.
    • Excessive flood: continuously sending messages or emojis in chat.
    • Constant use of caps: excessive use of capital letters.
    • Talking about other servers: promoting or discussing other servers within chat or Discord.

What is considered toxic behavior?

Toxic behavior is any conduct that repeatedly bothers or affects other players, even if it does not directly violate PvP rules.

  1. Repeated harassment: chasing or attacking the same user repeatedly, preventing them from playing normally.
  2. Taunting after a kill: mocking or laughing openly at a defeated player, especially if done repeatedly.
  3. Harassment by the killed player: responding with insults, taunts, or constant stalking.
  4. Scamming: tricking players into giving up items, money, or other valuable in-game resources.
  5. TPA Kill: using teleport requests to trick and kill a player unexpectedly.
  6. Repeatedly disconnecting during PvP: disconnecting multiple times to avoid PvP combat.

Buying VIP ranks does not exempt you from following the rules. If you break a rule, you will receive the same penalty as any other user.

If you have read the rules, you can go and enjoy the fun on our server!
